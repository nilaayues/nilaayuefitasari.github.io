INDONESIA

Indonesia is the largest archipelagic country in the world. Indonesia has more than 17,000 islands, of which only about 7,000 are inhabited. ... The capital city of Indonesia is Jakarta, which is located on the island of Java.

There are several forms of diversity in Indonesia, ranging from ethnic diversity, religious diversity, racial diversity, and also the diversity of group members.
